Angela's Recipe for the Best cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

for the dough

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3cups bread flour

for the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamons
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. mix the milk with the yeast sugar eggs
  2. melt the b utter and add to the mixture
  3. add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
  4. KSnead the dough for 10 minuites
